# ESC Battery
- Batteries must be connected in parallel
- Only use 6 cell batteries
- Make sure the voltages on the batteries match
- The battery capacity(mAh) must be close(within 100 mAh)
- Make sure polarities match when plugging them in
    - Red wires to Red, and Black to Black
- Batteries must be secure with velcro and strapped on tightly

# Propeller
- The propeller must be balanced
- Propeller must be bolted tightly onto the motor

# Electronics
- Only use 3 cell batteries with an xt-30 connector
- Polarities must match
    - Red wires to Red, and Black to Black

# Wire management
- Make sure all wires are secure with duct tape or zipties
- For connections to circuit boards, leave some slack for strain relief
